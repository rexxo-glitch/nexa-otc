How Security Works

Every NEXA OTC trade follows a strict manual verification process. You'll never be asked to send funds without a confirmed rate and a live operator on the line.

STEP 01
📡
Rate Confirmation
Live market rate is pulled from CoinGecko and displayed on-site. Your final OTC rate is confirmed by the operator on Telegram before any payment.
STEP 02
🔎
Manual Verification
Operator reviews your order details — asset, amount, and payment method — manually. No automated bots are involved in your trade.
STEP 03
💬
Telegram Communication
All coordination happens on a dedicated Telegram thread. Payment instructions, wallet addresses, and confirmations are shared directly and only with you.
STEP 04
🤝
Trade Confirmation
Both sides confirm before funds are released. The operator sends a receipt and trade confirmation once settlement is complete.
STEP 05
✅
Settlement & Receipt
Crypto is sent to your wallet or ETB transferred to your bank. A completed order summary is sent to you on Telegram. Done.
ALWAYS
🚫
What We Never Do
NEXA OTC will never ask for your seed phrase, private keys, or passwords. If anyone does — it is not us. Your funds stay yours.
